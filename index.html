Analysed 3 years of official NHS England data across 124 Trusts and 47 million A&E patient visits to investigate NHS constitutional performance. Built a complete ETL pipeline merging three annual government reports, standardising datasets, mapping all 124 Trusts to 7 NHS regions, writing 10 SQL queries, and delivering a 2-page interactive Power BI dashboard. The finding: not a single Trust out of 124 met the 95% target in any of the 3 years. 19.7 million patients waited longer than they constitutionally should have. My local Sunderland Trust performed at 55.07% below the national average.
