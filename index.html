Latest project · Healthcare
NHS England A&E Performance Analysis
View on GitHub ↗
End-to-end · Excel > MySQL > Power BI · Official NHS England Data
58.2%Avg 4hr performance
0/124Trusts hit 95% target
19.7MPatients failed
41.6%Of all patients

Analysed 3 years of official NHS England data across 124 Trusts and 47 million A&E patient visits to investigate whether the NHS is keeping its constitutional promise to see patients within 4 hours. The findings are stark not a single Trust out of 124 hit the 95% target in any of the 3 years analysed. Even after NHS England quietly lowered the target to 78% in March 2024, 109 out of 124 Trusts still failed. 19.7 million patients 41.6% of all A&E visits waited longer than they constitutionally should have. My local Trust in Sunderland performed at 55.07%, below the national average, meaning nearly half of all local patients are failed every day.
